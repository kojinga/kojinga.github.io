Services

Wordpress

We create beautiful and unique websites, using the latest web technology. We will keep your website up to date and protect it against malware. We make sure that people will be able to find your business online.

Security

As a business owner, it’s not only important to be online, but to stay online. That’s why it’s important to have the best protection you can get, in case something goes wrong.

If your website is infected with malware, we clean it and where possible, restore your data. We will help prevent data loss in the future by setting up regular backups.

Bitcoin

Bitcoin is a new generation of money. It enables you to send and receive money quickly, cheaply and securely. We’ll teach you how to safely accept Bitcoin payments on your website and in your local establishment.

We’ll show you how to use it every day, how to store it, spend it, and even how to back up your money safely. With Bitcoin, there are no more chargeback fees. When you receive a payment in Bitcoins, they can be immediately converted to Euros or Dollars and transferred to your regular bank account.
